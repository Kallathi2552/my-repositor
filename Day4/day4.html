Monkey D luffy

Luffy's crew, the Straw Hat Pirates, is a diverse group of adventurers seeking dreams while sailing with Captain Monkey D. Luffy, the future Pirate King, on their ship, the Thousand Sunny, with key members: swordsman Zoro, navigator Nami, marksman Usopp, cook Sanji, doctor Chopper, archaeologist Robin, cyborg shipwright Franky, skeleton musician Brook, and helmsman Jinbe, forming a loyal, powerful family that challenges the World Government.

zoro

Roronoa Zoro, known as "Pirate Hunter" Zoro, is the green-haired master swordsman and combatant for the Straw Hat Pirates in One Piece

Sanji

Sanji is the charismatic, suit-wearing, leg-kicking chef and a core member of the Straw Hat Pirates in One Piece, known for his chivalrous yet perverted obsession with women

jinbei

Jinbe is a large, blue whale shark fish-man, a master of Fish-Man Karate, and the current helmsman and a loyal member of the Straw Hat Pirates
